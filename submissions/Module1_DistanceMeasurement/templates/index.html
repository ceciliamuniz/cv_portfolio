Real-world Distance Measurement

Using Perspective Projection and Camera Calibration

How it works:

This application uses perspective projection mathematics and pre-calibrated camera intrinsic parameters to convert pixel coordinates into real-world distances. Simply click two points on an image to measure the actual distance between them in centimeters.
